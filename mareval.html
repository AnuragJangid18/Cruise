Who we are

Built on the
open sea

Mareval was born at the intersection of industrial precision and maritime luxury. We operate dual divisions — a global cargo network that moves goods across six continents, and a curated cruise charter service that transforms ocean travel into an art form.

With 42 vessels, 174 port connections, and over 12,000 completed voyages, we carry the weight of commerce and the lightness of leisure with equal mastery.
